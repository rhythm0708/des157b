Example 2: Earth 2050

A speculative website containing short articles and images depicting what people might expect to see in the next few decades

  • Beautiful artworks that really sell the idea that we're looking at a future earth with limitless possibilities.
  • Below-average loading times and buffering.
  • Readers can engage with the website by voting on whether or not they think each prediction will come true.
  • To vote, readers need to sign up on the website.
  • Futuristic vibe conveyed through a vibrant color scheme
